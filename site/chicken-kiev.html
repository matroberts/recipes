Chicken Kiev

This recipe is from the Hello Cookbook by Jill Cox. Serves 4.
Serves 4
100 gButter
1/2 Lemon, juice and zest
4Garlic cloves, minced
2 tbspTarragon, finely chopped
Salt and Pepper
4Chicken Breasts
Flour
2Eggs beaten
Panko Breadcrumbs
Make the tarragon butter. Mash the butter, then beat in lemon juice, zest, garlic, tarragon, salt and pepper. Roll the butter into a tube, wrap in cling film, and put in fridge. Prepare the chicken. Gently beat the chicken breast flat with a rolling pin. Season with salt and pepper on both sides. Put a line of tarragon butter down the centre of each breast. Roll up the breasts, and hold closed with a bunch of cocktail sticks. You want to try and seal the butter completely inside the chicken breast. Dip each breast in flour, then the beaten egg, then the panko breadcrumbs.
Mat Roberts
