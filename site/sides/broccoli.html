Broccoli

Broccoli is not nice when it's overcooked, so be careful to not overdo it - it only takes about 3 minutes. The water should be heavily salted.
Serves 4
Broccoli
Salt

Bring a large pan of heavily salted water to the boil. Cut the florets off the broccoli. Add broccoli to the pan, and cook for about 3 mins. Cover the pan at the beginning to quickly re-establish the boil, but then remove the lid. To test for doneness, stab the thick end of the floret with a fork. Drain in a colander.
